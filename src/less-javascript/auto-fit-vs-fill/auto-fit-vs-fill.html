auto-fit vs auto-fill

Same repeat(?, minmax(200px, 1fr)). Different word. Resize the window or change the item count to see how they diverge.

auto-fill

Reserves columns for items that aren’t there. Leftover space stays empty.

repeat(auto-fill, minmax(200px, 1fr))

auto-fit

Collapses empty tracks. Existing items stretch to fill the row.

repeat(auto-fit, minmax(200px, 1fr))
